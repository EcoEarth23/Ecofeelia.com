How are Contribution & Points Calculated?

Contribution Formula:

Contribution = (CO₂ Reduced × 10) + (Plastic Rescued × 5) + (Trees Planted × 50)

Points Formula:

Points = Contribution ÷ 10

Example:

If a company reduces 80 kg CO₂, rescues 50 kg plastic, and plants 10 trees:

Contribution = (80 × 10) + (50 × 5) + (10 × 50) = 1450

Points = 1450 ÷ 10 = 145
